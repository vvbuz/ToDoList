How to use to-do list

Type the text of your task in the field below. After you push the button, your task will be added to the task list. If you have finished your task, just click at the 'delete' button against your task. The task will be marked as complete and will be gone. If you want to change the text of your task so click on 'edit' button against your task then put the new text of task and click on 'Save' button.
